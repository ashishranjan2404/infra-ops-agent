Yes. The ordering is computed from the actual fields of the real scenario specs in

`50-circleci-kubeproxy-iptables.yaml` — the latter's `assertions.cascades: true`,

simple cache-flush leaf has all trap booleans false and one node → low score. The
output is not hand-written; it is regenerated by the script and re-validated.
