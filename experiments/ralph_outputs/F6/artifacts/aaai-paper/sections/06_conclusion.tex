\section{Conclusion}
\label{sec:conclusion}

We presented \textsc{SRE-Degrees}, a verifiable environment for safe incident remediation
under cascading failure, a root-cause-aware reward with an explicit trap penalty, and a
searched, rules-as-data safety verifier that generalizes to held-out incidents. An honest
ablation relocates the contribution to the environment and verifier rather than the
refinement loop. We release 51 scenarios, a deterministic judge, and FIREBALL-schema
trajectories with genuine within-group spread, in the spirit of: \emph{you can improve models
at anything you can verify}.

\paragraph{Reproducibility.}
Code, scenario specs, the deterministic judge, and trajectory data accompany the paper.
Tier-A is fully deterministic and free; Tier-B requires a GKE cluster.
